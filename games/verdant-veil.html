Inspiration

As a Photography enthusiasts passionate about capturing the natural scenery, I deeply appreciate the solace that nature brings to our soul, something unattainable in industrialized cities. Earth is a miracle in the vast universe. I'll never forget the desire to find terrestrial planets when playing "No man's sky". I believe the earth will always be the Eden for human beings. However, in the 21st century, environmental conservation becomes a tool in political struggles, it seems the word is willing to ignore the harms should have been eliminated…

Concept

Main Character

Lia

Lia, embarks on a destined journey, restore the balance against the encroaching of Zenthrax. She rescued a firefly called "Lumina" affected by the Zenthrax fell in the woods.

Lumina

Lumina illuminate darkness, reveal hidden paths, and activate the light-sensitive mechanisms. It's also the expand of Natural's Whisper.

Echo

Echo can emit a series of soundwaves, visualized for the player as waves that bounce off surfaces and reveal the cave's topography, finding hidden openings, or avoiding collapses in the unstable cave.

Muelsyse

Muelsyse can disguise to stealthily navigate areas heavily monitored by the Zeners and their drones.
