About the Game

Myth Valley is an adventure into a forgotten world where folklore comes alive. Explore ancient ruins, uncover hidden stories, and forge alliances with mythical spirits as you work to restore balance to the valley.

Key Features

Living Legends: Encounter spirits inspired by global mythologies, each with their own quests and abilities.

Dynamic Ecosystems: Shift the environment through choices that alter weather, flora, and the creatures you meet.

Exploration & Puzzles: Traverse vertical cliffs, shimmering lakes, and forgotten temples while solving environmental riddles.

Gameplay

Guide your explorer through biomes that respond to the artifacts you awaken. Master traversal tools, harness companion powers, and uncover the valley's evolving mysteries. Every region offers new mechanics and secrets to reward curious players.

Development

Myth Valley is crafted as an atmospheric narrative adventure. Our team focused on seamless environmental storytelling, expressive companion AI, and a soundtrack that adapts to player choices. The result is a journey that feels intimate, mysterious, and continually surprising.
